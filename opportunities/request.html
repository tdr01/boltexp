Comments/Questions

To become an approved carrier for Bolt Express you must meet the following:

  • MC/MX# or DOT# to begin registration.
    • Intrastate carriers without a DOT# may provide an applicable state registration number to begin registration.
    • Contract Carrier Authority only
  • Valid W-9
  • Provide a Certificate of Insurance that meets the insurance minimum requirements below.
    • Cargo $100,000
    • Auto $1,000,000
    • General Liability $1,000,000 *Preferred/not required
  • Carrier must have a safety rating of Satisfactory or None
    • If rated None, the carrier must have less than 3 BASIC scores above threshold.
  • 24 hour operations are preferred!
    • After hours contacts or driver contact information for carriers who are not staffed
    • Macro Point tracking is mandatory for carriers not staffed 24 hours.
