About Us

Bolt Express was started in 2001 after acquiring the assets of a small expedite carrier. With four employees and eleven trucks, a business plan was developed by Ben Bauman. That same business plan has remained the foundation of Bolt Express since its inception. Today, Bolt Express is the largest privately held expedite transportation company in the country. The success of Bolt Express can be attributed to a focus on three key business concepts: Providing Exceptional Customer Service, Maintaining Strong Partnerships with our Owner Operators and an Aggressive Growth Strategy.

Commitment to Quality

Our commitment to our Quality Objectives is best defined in our Quality Policy: Bolt Express is committed to satisfying our customers' expectations. We pledge to be responsive and follow through on all commitments. We are predictable, reliable, trustworthy, safety conscious and willing to go the extra mile for something we believe in. We are committed to continual improvement because we want to be the best at what we do.

Commitment to Health and Wellness

Bolt Express is committed to the health and wellness of our employees. We have defined a program with representatives in all offices to organize health programs, fitness activities, encourage healthy food choices and promote wellness. It is our commitment to our team, both personally and professionally, to maintain a positive influence to their overall quality of life.
